EXHIBIT B — SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

B.1 Availability Commitment. AcmeCorp commits to a monthly uptime of ninety-nine point nine percent (99.9%) for the production environment of the Services, measured on a calendar-month basis (the "Uptime Commitment"). Uptime is calculated as: (Total Minutes in Month − Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month × 100. "Downtime" means a period during which the production environment of the Services is unavailable to all ContosoAI Authorized Users, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Excused Outages as defined below.

B.2 Scheduled Maintenance. AcmeCorp may perform scheduled maintenance on the Services during maintenance windows of up to four (4) hours per calendar month, occurring during low-usage periods (typically Saturday or Sunday between 2:00 a.m. and 6:00 a.m. Pacific Time). AcmeCorp shall provide ContosoAI with at least seventy-two (72) hours' advance notice of scheduled maintenance, and shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize service disruption. Scheduled maintenance time shall not count as Downtime.

B.3 Excused Outages. The following events shall not constitute Downtime for purposes of calculating uptime: (a) Force Majeure Events; (b) ContosoAI's acts or omissions, including introduction of Harmful Code or misuse of the Services; (c) outages attributable to third-party services not within AcmeCorp's reasonable control (e.g., public internet backbone outages, third-party SaaS provider outages); (d) outages resulting from ContosoAI's custom integrations or modifications; or (e) outages during the initial setup or migration period prior to the Go-Live Date.

B.4 Service Credits. If AcmeCorp fails to meet the Uptime Commitment in any calendar month, ContosoAI shall be eligible to receive a service credit against future invoices according to the following schedule:

    Monthly Uptime of 99.0% to 99.8%: Credit equal to 5% of the monthly Subscription Fee.
    Monthly Uptime of 95.0% to 98.99%: Credit equal to 10% of the monthly Subscription Fee.
    Monthly Uptime below 95.0%: Credit equal to 25% of the monthly Subscription Fee.

Service credits are ContosoAI's sole and exclusive remedy for any failure by AcmeCorp to meet the Uptime Commitment. Credits shall not exceed 25% of the monthly Subscription Fee in any calendar month. Credits may not be redeemed for cash.

B.5 Credit Request Process. To receive a service credit, ContosoAI must submit a written credit request to AcmeCorp's support team within thirty (30) days of the end of the calendar month in which the downtime occurred, providing reasonable documentation of the outage. AcmeCorp shall respond to credit requests within fifteen (15) business days.

B.6 Recovery Objectives. AcmeCorp's target recovery objectives for the Services in the event of a disaster are: Recovery Time Objective (RTO): four (4) hours; Recovery Point Objective (RPO): one (1) hour. These are targets, not guarantees, and do not form part of the Uptime Commitment.
