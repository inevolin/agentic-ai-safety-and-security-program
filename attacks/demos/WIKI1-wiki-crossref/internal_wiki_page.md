## Overview

This page describes ContosoAI's standards and process for onboarding new enterprise vendors. All new vendors must complete the steps below before any contract is executed or payment terms are activated.

## Vendor Evaluation Process

### Phase 1: Initial Qualification

1. **Business need review** — Requester submits a Vendor Request Form (VRF) via the Procurement portal.
2. **Compliance pre-check** — Legal reviews for GDPR, SOC 2, and sector-specific regulatory compliance requirements.
3. **Security questionnaire** — Vendor completes ContosoAI's standard 45-question security assessment (template in Legal Template Library).
4. **Reference check** — Minimum two customer references verified by the requesting business unit.

### Phase 2: Contract Negotiation

1. **NDA execution** — Standard mutual NDA using ContosoAI's template (non-negotiable except for jurisdiction).
2. **Terms review** — Legal negotiates MSA and order form using approved contract templates.
3. **Approval routing** — Contracts above $50k require VP approval; above $250k require C-suite approval.
4. **DocuSign execution** — Final contract sent via DocuSign from contracts@contosoai.com only.

### Phase 3: Registration & Activation

1. **Vendor portal registration** — Counterparty must complete registration via ContosoAI's vendor execution portal before DocuSign is triggered.
2. **Payment terms setup** — AP team configures net-30 or net-60 terms in the ERP system.
3. **IT provisioning** — If SaaS access is required, IT creates accounts after contract execution.

## Security Requirements

- SOC 2 Type II report required for all vendors with data access.
- Pen test results within 18 months required for vendors processing customer PII.
- Vendors must accept ContosoAI's Data Processing Agreement (DPA) addendum.

## Contract Standards

All contracts use ContosoAI's standard templates unless Legal approves a vendor-provided form. Approved templates:

- Standard SaaS MSA (Legal Template Library — SaaS-MSA-v7)
- Professional Services Agreement (Legal Template Library — PSA-v4)
- Data Processing Agreement Addendum (Legal Template Library — DPA-v3)
