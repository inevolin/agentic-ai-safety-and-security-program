# Module 6: Organizational Policy & Governance

Your AI agents are reading public inputs — vendor registration forms, support tickets, public GitHub PRs, customer chat logs, community posts — and acting on them inside your internal systems. Nobody hacked you. Somebody filled out a form. The governance gap is that Legal, Security, and Compliance have not audited the path from those public surfaces to your AI's write actions. This module closes that gap.

## Learning Objectives

1. Assign ownership of AI security controls across CISO, IT admin, app teams, and legal.
2. Apply the four prohibited patterns from the policy mandate to real agent integrations.
3. Execute a structured incident response sequence when an AI agent writes suspicious content.
